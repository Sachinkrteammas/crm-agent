14. Oil Separation Issue in Peanut Butter
Reason

 :
´éÀ
Oil separation in peanut butter occurs due to the natural oils present
in peanuts, rising to the top over time.
´éÀ
Temperature fluctuations and improper storage conditions can
further contribute to this issue.
´éÀ
Additionally, natural peanut butter, which often lacks stabilizers, is
more susceptible to separation.
Solution:
´éÀ
To fix it, just stir the peanut butter to mix the oil back in before you use it.
Keeping peanut butter jar in the refrigerator, can slow down oil separation.
