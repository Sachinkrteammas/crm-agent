Capture A Trip – Call Handling Script

Opening Greeting:
- "Capture A Trip! How may I help you today?"

For New Enquiries:
- "Thank you for showing interest in Capture A Trip. May I please take a few details to assist you better?"
Details to Capture:
  • Full Name
  • Destination or Location
  • Month of Travel / Date of Travel
  • Number of Travelers
  • Any Specific Package preference
  • Additional Remarks
  • Mobile Number
Response (to Caller):
- "Thank you. I have shared your enquiry with our support team. They will get back to you within 30 minutes."
Special Notes:
- After 7pm: Do not commit TAT, always say "Our support team will get in touch with you shortly."
- Before 10am and after 7pm: same as above.

For Existing Bookings:
- "May I please have your Registered Mobile Number?"
- Confirm customer name.
Response (to Caller):
- "Thank you. I have raised your query with our support team. They will get back to you within 2 hours."
